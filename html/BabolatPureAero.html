Babolat Pure Aero

The Babolat Pure Aero is designed for aggressive baseline players who want explosive spin, power, and modern feel.

Specifications

  • Head size: 100 sq in / 645 cm2
  • Balance: 32.99 cm (approx 4 pts Head Light)
  • Length: 27 in / 68.58 cm
  • Beam Width: 23 / 26 / 23 mm
  • String Pattern: 16 × 19
  • Material Composition: Graphite (NF²-Tech & FSI Spin Technology)
  • Weight (unstrung): 300 g / 10.6 oz
  • Stiffness: ~69 RA
Pros Cons
Massive spin potential
Powerful baseline performance
Excellent maneuverability 		Can feel stiff for some players
